EasyHisab Account Deletion

Last updated: April 21, 2026

How to Request Deletion

Users can request account deletion by emailing easyhisabsupport@gmail.com.

Please include:

  • Registered email

What Data Is Deleted

  • User account
  • Business details
  • Invoices and customer data

Deletion Policy

  • Soft delete is applied immediately after request verification
  • Permanent deletion within 30 days

Retention Note

Some data may be retained for a limited duration if required for legal obligations or GST compliance.

Confirmation

Users receive a confirmation email once deletion is completed.

Need help with deletion status? Email easyhisabsupport@gmail.com.
