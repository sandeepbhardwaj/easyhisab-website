Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

EasyHisab values your privacy and handles billing data with a security-first approach. This policy explains what data we collect, why it is used, and what rights you have.

  • To support analytics and product improvements, if analytics tools are enabled

Data Sharing

EasyHisab does not sell personal data to third parties.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS to protect communication between app and backend systems.

User Rights

You can request account and data deletion at any time.
